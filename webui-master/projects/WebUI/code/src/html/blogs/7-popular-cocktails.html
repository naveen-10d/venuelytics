7 Popular Cocktails Every Bartender and Cocktail Enthusiast Must Know

When we talk about drinks at bars, nightclubs and pubs, it is not just about hard alcohol and beer. It is about the new trends. We discovered that more and more people these days prefer exotic and colorful cocktails.

Before we begin, let us clear the difference between cocktails and mocktails. So cocktails are alcoholic drinks made by mixing alcoholic beverages with soft drinks, fruit juices or other alcohols. Mocktails are non-alcoholic drinks created by combining different fruit juices, iced tea or soft drinks.

Some of the cocktails were created over a century ago. Some are well-known favorites of all time, and some have evolved over the years. Creative bartenders are due credit for creating colorful and exotic cocktails.
