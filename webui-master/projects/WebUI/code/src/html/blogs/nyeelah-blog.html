How challenging is it to be a nightlife promoter?

Nyeelah

Being a promoter is extremely challenging, especially now since the business has become so saturated with sub promoters, everything has become about money, cover charge at most of the upscale manhattan nightclubs has escalated from $20 to $60/$80 per person or large groups are required to buy bottles..bottle service is literally killing NYC Nightlife

What can you tell us about nightlife trends in NYC?

Nyeelah

The growing trend is that nightclubs are now paying hot girls to hangout in the nightclubs this has been happening for a while but now it's at its peak..60% of the hot girls in the nightclubs are paid to be there.. which isn't fun for them and it's not what nightlife is about to the blind eye no one would be able to tell but I can spot it from a mile away..never the less I am in love with NYC Nightlife. I have made some amazing connections and friendships that have changed me and my life.. because of NYC Nightlife I have promoted the hottest Nightclubs in Paris France and Los Angeles California..
