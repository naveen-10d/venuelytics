How to Promote Your Nightlife Venue to The Millennials

Unlike Gen X, Millennials are between the ages of 18-35 of age and majority are about interaction social media and connecting over the smart phones. They can multitask, make fast decisions and rely heavily on technology and the internet. Overall, they enjoy sharing their experiences with friends in a digital world more so than other generations.

Their lifestyle, values, and trends are also different. They may not be big spenders but they pay more for fast service and the “experience”. One industry that has been slow to react to the new generation market is the nightlife industry. To better serve the Millennials, venues must adapt and to please this new growing market.

Let us summarize the more important characteristics of the new Generation so Venus can better serve them.

1. Living life in the fast lane

2. Risk taking and adventurous

3. Passionate learners and explorers

4. Spend most of their time on smartphones

5. More technology oriented and believe in automation

6. Like art and creativity, and more likely to get tattoos and body piercings.

7. Multitasking and with precision

Some critical factors about Millennials and their grading/rating of the nightclub, bar, or any nightlife venue.

1. Millennials are excellent researchers, they check out the nightclub online for more information, and they don't just choose the venue because it is flashy and trendy, they prefer an experience. So, create the right ambiance and be authentic to offer the right experience.

2. Millennials are digitally connected. They are the first set of super users of mobile devices, and hence a nightlife venue should utilize the right tools to market to the Millennials on their mobile devices or social media channels.

3. Millennials want to be engaged, so ditch hard selling and connect with them directly.

4. Millennials are highly passionate and digitally skillful, so use the best digital-marketing tools to reach them directly.

5. Once Millennials approve your product/service, in this case, a nightlife venue, it is sure to go viral and reach many potential customers.


Improve your digital-marketing efforts, test and try different tactics that align with Millennials interests. Moreover, try to build credibility and be authentic, and with little patience, you will see positive results.
