New Orleans Nightlife and Bottle Service

If you are looking for a unique Jazz infuse cultural experience than you must visit New Orleans. In many ways it is an authentic historical city with an amazing charming nightlife and diverse culture.

There are casinos, clubs, music/karaoke bars, late night spots, etc. Aside the Mardi Gras, one of the most popular events in New Orleans, the city is also full of mysterious alley ways with hidden nightlife treasures to explore.

Some of the attractions to name are below,

  • Irish Pubs - Finn McCool’s, The Irish House, Mick’s Irish Pub, Erin Rose, Tracy’s are some of the bars you must visit.
  • Live Jazz - If you are into traditional live Jazz music visit Snug Harbor.
  • Monteleone Hotel Carousel Bar is also great place to visit.
